State of Tennessee, Attorney III from January 2013-Present

I provide legal advice to the Board for Licensing Contractors to ensure that it performs its licensing application review, disciplinary complaint review and rulemaking functions in accordance with their authorizing statutes and administrative rules. In fulfillment of these duties, I oversee the effecient review and resolution of the Board's case and complaint registeries. I also review and recommend best responses to legal inquiries for licensees, industry groups and governmental assemblies to the Board. I previously performed the same duties for four (4) other Boards and Commissions before being assigned to the Contractors Board in August 2014.
